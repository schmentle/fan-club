About the Show

The Big Bang Theory is an American sitcom centered around a group of friends — brilliant scientists and their social circle — blending humor, science, and geek culture.

Sheldon Cooper

A theoretical physicist with a genius-level IQ but little social awareness.

Leonard Hofstadter

An experimental physicist and roommate of Sheldon, often the peacekeeper of the group.

Penny

A waitress and aspiring actress who lives across the hall, bringing a normal perspective to the group.

Howard Wolowitz

A quirky aerospace engineer known for his bold fashion and living with his mother for many years.

Rajesh Koothrappali

An astrophysicist originally unable to talk to women without alcohol.

Amy Farrah Fowler

A neuroscientist introduced later, who becomes Sheldon’s girlfriend and eventually wife.

Bernadette Rostenkowski

A microbiologist with a surprisingly fierce personality, married to Howard.
